    Tuna and lettuce salad

      Ingredients

    • 1 can of Tuna in water
    • 1/2 cucumber
    • 1 1/2 cup of Lechuga of the kind you like or watercress
    • 5 cherry tomatoes
    • 1 lemon

      Preparation

    1. wash your ingredients with plenty of water.
    2. cut your cucumber into not very thick slices and then cut each slice in two.
    3. cut your tomato in two or three pieces and sprinkle a pinch of salt.
    4. cut the lettuce or watercress in small pieces or as you like.
