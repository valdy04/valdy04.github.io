    Watercress and meatballs

      Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 cup Watercress or any kind of lettuce you like
    • 1/2 Tomato
    • 1/4Onion
    • Roasted beef in the shape of a meatball

      • For the meatballs

    • 1/2 cup of ground beef
    • 1/4 onion
    • Sal, pepper and oregano to taste.

      Preparation

    1. wash your ingredients with plenty of water.
    2. cut the watercress or lettuce and get them to the size you like.
    3. cut the onion into strips.
    4. cut the tomato into small cubes and sprinkle a pinch of salt.
    5. in a container put first the lettuce or watercress, the the tomatoes and the onions.

      6. For the meatballs

    6. cut the onion into very small pieces.
    7. mix all the ingredients.
    8. With the help of a spoon, take small portions and form small balls with the meat.
    9. if you are going to use a pan to cook the meatballs use a non-stick spray and cook them over medium heat. If you are going to use an oven or air fryer, place the meatballs in a non-stick container.

    Enjoy the result and share with friends.
