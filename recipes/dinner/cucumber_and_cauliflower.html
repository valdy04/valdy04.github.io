    Cucumber and cauliflower

      Ingredients

    • 1 cauliflower, cleaned and cut into florets
    • 1 large can sweet corn, drained and rinsed
    • 1 larga cucumber, sliced ​​and halved or quartered
    • ½ bell pepper (any color), diced
    • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions
    • ½ cup mayonnaise or plain Greek yogurt
    • I tablespoon chopped fresh dill
    • 1 1/2 cup iceberg lettuce (or any kind you like)
    • Salt and pepper, to taste

      Preparation

    1. In a bowl, whisk mayonnaise (or yogurt) with dill, salt and pepper. Set aside.
    2. In a large bowl, toss cauliflower, sweet corn, and bell pepper.
    3. Pour dressing over top and toss until all ingredients are well blended. Top (and toss again, if you want to) with green onion.
    4. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

    Enjoy the result and share with friends.
