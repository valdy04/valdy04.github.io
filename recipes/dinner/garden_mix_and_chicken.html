    Garden lettuce mix and chicken breast

      Ingredients

    • 1 medium chicken breast
    • Salt and pepper (for seasoning)
    • 1 lemon
    • 2 cups garden lettuce mix
    • 4 cherry tomatoes
    • 1/2 cucumber
    • Dressing salad (optional and any kind you like)

      Preparation

    1. wash the chicken breast with a little lemon and season with salt and pepper on both sides.
    2. In a frying pan over medium heat, use a little non-stick Dorantes and brown the breast on both sides. You can add a little more lemon if you like.
    3. when the breast is ready cut it into strips or cubes.
    4. wash the lettuce with water, drain them and put them in a bowl.
    5. cut the cherry tomatoes into two or three pieces each and sprinkle a pinch of salt.
    6. cut the cucumber into slices and then cut each slice in two.
    7. mix all the ingredients of the salad and place the chicken in the middle.

    Enjoy the result and share with friends.
